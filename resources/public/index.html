Development & Design Meetup • Fresno, CA

Geeks Being Geeks

Undefined is a group geeks with a passion for development, design and technology. We meet once a month and have a member speak on a topic they are interested in. There is no agenda or format, just a platform for others to share their knowledge and passions. We encourage healthy debate and grabbing beers after the meetup.

We are language agnostic and encourage sharing of whatever the speakers is passionate about. Topics can range from a what the speaker has built, tips and tricks, things they learned from failing, or something they are experimenting with. All are welcome, so join us at our next meetup and meet some awesome people.
